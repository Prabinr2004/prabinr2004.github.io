About Me

I'm currently at the College of Idaho, pursuing a double major in Computer Science & Mathematics and Digital Marketing, with a minor in Arts. It's an unconventional combination, but it reflects exactly how I think — I'm drawn to problems that sit at the intersection of technology, creativity, and human experience. My academic focus leans toward Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing, and I've maintained a 3.768 GPA with Dean's List recognition three years running.


One of my deepest long-term goals is to build a language model for Nepali. Most of the world's AI is built around a handful of dominant languages, and I believe that's a gap worth closing. Beyond coursework, I've worked on statistical research projects analyzing real-world data, explored data visualization and analytics, and continuously look for opportunities to apply what I learn to problems that actually matter. I'm currently seeking research and graduate opportunities that push me further into applied ML and data science.


I'm a quick learner, a collaborative teammate, and someone who genuinely enjoys the process of figuring things out — whether that's debugging code, analyzing a dataset, or building something from scratch.
